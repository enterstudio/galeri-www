Turn any screen into an art exhibit.

Galeri exists to dutifully bring you a steady stream of fresh artwork: regionally and globally sourced, old and new, cage-free and organic.

But we digress.

The point is that if you're looking for little inspirations throughout the day, want to learn more about art history, or are just bored with having to choose your computer's background, then you may like what you get when you give Galeri a shot.

Galeri is free, and always will be.

Galeri pulls open content from around the world, and aims to follow the spirit of its sources by never putting up any sort of gateway or obstacle between you and something that should never be used for profit.

Special thanks to Wikipedia, Rijksmuseum, The Metropolitan Museum of Art, and The Cooper Hewitt Museum of Design for providing the open material that make projects like this possible.
